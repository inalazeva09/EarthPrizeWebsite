About the Project

The Healthcare Algae Air Purifier is a nature-inspired system designed to improve indoor air quality in hospitals and clinics. Air quality is a critical factor in healthcare environments, where patients and staff spend extended periods of time.

Our design explores the use of algae as a natural component for air purification. Algae naturally absorb carbon dioxide and contribute to balanced air conditions in ecosystems, making them an interesting and sustainable option for indoor environments.

Overall Product / Idea

Air Purification

The system uses algae to help cleanse the air by absorbing carbon dioxide and supporting cleaner indoor conditions.

Smart Monitoring

Sensors monitor air quality, temperature, and carbon dioxide levels to detect changes in environmental conditions.

Automated Response

When pollutant levels increase, the purifier activates automatically to improve air circulation and quality.

Expansion Plan

Our long-term goal is to introduce this purifier into hospitals and clinics where air quality plays a major role in comfort and safety. Healthcare environments constantly seek improvements that support patient well-being.

With continued development and testing, the system could be produced on a larger scale and implemented in multiple healthcare settings. This includes patient areas, waiting rooms, and other indoor spaces where air quality is essential.

Using algae as a renewable resource also supports sustainability and explores alternatives to conventional purification systems.
